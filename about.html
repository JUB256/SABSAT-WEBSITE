About SABSAT CONTRACTORS

Leading the way in architectural and engineering excellence across Uganda

Our Story

SABSAT CONTRACTORS AND SUPPLIERS LTD is a leading architecture and civil engineering firm based in Kabale Municipality, Uganda. Founded on a passion for innovative design and quality construction, we deliver exceptional services to clients across the region.

Our team of experienced professionals combines creativity, technical expertise, and project management skills to bring projects to life ensuring swift and detailed implementation in construction matters.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge architectural and engineering solutions that exceed client expectations, while promoting sustainable development and community growth.

Our Vision

To be the preferred partner for architectural and engineering services in Uganda, recognized for our excellence, integrity, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Experience

SABSAT CONTRACTORS AND SUPPLIERS LTD has completed numerous projects across various sectors

Residential Buildings

From single-family homes to multi-unit residential complexes, we create comfortable and functional living spaces that meet modern lifestyle needs.

Commercial Complexes

We design and construct commercial buildings that enhance business operations while creating attractive and functional workspaces.

Institutional Facilities

Our expertise extends to schools, churches, and other institutional buildings that serve community needs and promote social development.
